Rotary's Areas of Focus

The work of Rotary begins in the community and every community has its own unique needs and concerns. While we serve in countless ways, we've focused our efforts in six key areas to maximize our impact. These areas encompass some of the world's most critical and widespread humanitarian needs and we have a proven record of success in addressig them:

Peace and Conflict Prevention / Resolution

Disease prevention and treatment

Water and Sanitation

Maternal and Child Health

Basic Edutcation and Leteracy

Economic and Community Development
