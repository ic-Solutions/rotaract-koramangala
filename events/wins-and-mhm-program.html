WinS and MHM Program

Date

20th January 2020

Time

09:00 AM to 11:00 AM

Venue

Mitralaya Girls High School

Attendees

170 Students

Members : 4
Speaker for the session - 🗣

Rtn. Dr. Meenakshi Bharath

Event details:📝

-> All the students assembled and sang a few songs for us which was followed by an introduction to Rotary International, WinS and the Speaker by Rtn. Preetham. 🙌🏽

-> The speaker talked to us about Wash in Schools, the importance of following proper hygiene habits which was followed by a demonstration of correct way of hand washing by the Hand Washing ambassadors of the school and the volunteers. 🧴✋🏼🧼

-> The WinS session was followed by Menstrual Hygiene Management program where the girls learned about maintenance of good health during Period time. Dr. Meenakshi also gave a small talk about Sustainable Menstruation and demonstrated how to use a Cloth Pad. 🌸
