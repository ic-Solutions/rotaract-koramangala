Flow of Activities

~ Wetlands act as the first line of defense to cope against the adverse effects of climate change. We had a good turnout in our walkathon and we managed to create awareness on the importance of wetlands through the walkathon and interactive discussion.

~ We were also glad to see the general public showing interest about wetlands. We further recognized all the participants of the Step Out Live It 2.0 who completed and handed their award and all e certificate were distributed the same day. The fitness initiative was successfully completed after the 15 days.

~ Each participant assured us that they will spread the word to many more people and create awareness. Overall the project was very impactful as we managed to change the perspective of few individuals. Awareness is the first step towards conservation and we are glad that we have taken the first step today.
