Reminiscence V. 6.0

Date

15th February 2020

Time

07:00 PM to 10:00 PM

Venue

N.R. Hall, Koramangala Club

Attendees

35

Event Details: 📑

🌹 The event began with a welcome speech.🏽🏽

🌹 This was then followed by a couple game - Protect your Balloon 🎈🎈won by Rtn. Manivarnan and his wife.

🌹 Followed by the creative game - Express your Love on Paper 🖍🖌 where the Rotarians were provided with different materials and asked to express their married life on paper.

🌹 A romantic Ramp Walk 💃 by the couples.

🌹 The Rotarians explained the Art Work 📝 and their beautiful words of love filled the air. ❤💌

🌹 The couples were given innovative Impromptu Tasks and it was sportingly implemented.🌹

🌹 The winners in various categories such as Best Dressed Male 👔 and Best Dressed Woman 👗,were awarded. 🏆

🎁 Rtn. Preetham and his wife were crowned Mr. And Mrs. Valentine.🥇 The event was followed by Fellowship and Dinner. 🍽🥢 along with Cake Cutting 🎂🍰 to celebrate the Rotary - Rotaract Partnership.

🎁 The Rotaractors exchanged their gifts on the account of Secret Valentine. 👫🏻
