Learn with Fun

Date

8th September 2019

Time

11:00 AM to 05:00 PM

Venue

Rtr. Anurag's place

Attendees

18

Details of the event

1. Suicide Awareness session by Rtr. Shylaja She spoke about the symptoms of suicidal & awareness regarding suicide therapy. There were few related activities in this session making each of us felt better about ourselves post the session.

2. Personality Development and Dinning etiquette👩🏻‍🎓 by Anjalee She spoke about how the personality of each individual, their behavior, body language, gestures, first impression and attire makes a difference. 🧐🕴🏻 Dinning etiquette🍽 We learnt how to use spoon, fork🍴 and chopsticks🥢 to have a meal. We challenged each others' chopsticks skills with a fun peanut picking with chopsticks game. 🥜🏼

3. Scrumptious Lunch was prepared by Rtr. Venkatesh, Rtr. Bhaskar & Rtr. Syed. The menu included : 1. Veg Biriyani 2. Paneer Butter Masala, 3. Maddur Vadda 4. Raita 🍲🍛🥘😋

4. Bleed Green Session by Rtr. Aastha, Rtr. Monisha Clarification on the myths related to Menstruation and Menstrual Hygiene. 🚫 Awareness and introduction of Sustainable Menstrual products & Instructions on Menstrual Hygiene was given. 🏃🏼‍♀

5. Art workshop by Venkatesh We made Origami House, Roses, Butterfly, Snake, etc and finally ended with an RCK colony 🏘💐🦋

~ Star Rotaractors Recognition ✨ Rtr. Aastha for July ✨ Rtr. Bhaskar for August Our Past Presidents Rtr. Bharath & Rtr. Ravi handed gifts to them and spoke a few words of encouragement for all✨👏🏻
