Independence Day – School Visits

Date

15th August 2017

Time

NA

Venue

Franciscan School

Attendees

13

Club Attendees:

Rtr. Ravi, Rtr. Bharath, Rtr. Rachel, Rtr. Anurag, Rtr. Vaishnavi, Rtr. Shubham, Rtr. Trisha and Rtr. Ratish

Flow of Activities

~ Rotaractors divided themselves in two teams, one composed of Rtr. Ravi, Rtr. Bharath, Rtr. Rachel, Rtr. Anurag and Rtr. Vaishnavi went to Franciscan School where team interacted with the school kids, spend time with them and distributed ladoos.

~ Rtr. Shubham, Rtr. Trisha and Rtr. Ratish went to Chembenahalli Govt Primary School where various cultural events, including dance, singing, speech were given by school kids reminding all of us sacrifices made by our freedom fighters. Mementos were present to all three Rotaractors for being part of the event.
