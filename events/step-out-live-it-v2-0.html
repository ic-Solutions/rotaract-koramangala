Flow of Activities

~ Rtr. Ganesh took the initiative of making everyone fit with the coming New Year. Intention was to make this year a Happy Active Year in the start.

~ The 15 Days - 50KM challenge was planned in a way to make it achievable for all. Minimum distance to follow was : 20 Jan - 1.0km21 Jan - 1.5km22 Jan - 1.9km23 Jan - 2.5km24 Jan - 3.2km26 Jan - 3.5km26 Jan - 3.7km27 Jan - 3.8km28 Jan - 4.0km29 Jan - 4.2km30 Jan - 4.3km31 Jan - 4.6km1 Feb - 3.3km2 Feb - 3.5km3 Feb - 5.0km - Final day

~ Every week Members are sent the screenshot of run status and some time they also shared live running activity also posted in the group for live cheer using the “Runtastic app”. Members also used other fitness apps and sent the screenshot/pics of their every activities.

~ Step Out. Live it v2.0 has doubled the participation from first edition. Project had created very positive and fitness awareness to all & there is a plan to come up with a third edition of the event.
