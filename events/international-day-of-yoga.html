~ Mr. Suresh volunteered to conduct this event on the behalf of Isha foundation, a non-profit, spiritual organization founded in 1992 by Sadhguru Jaggi Vasudev.

~ During the event, we were informed about yoga and how it helps build strength, awareness and harmony in both mind & body.

~ We were also guided for doing breathing exercises, meditation and poses designed to encourage relaxation & reduce stress.

~ The event was followed by Dinner sponsored by Rotary Koramangala.
