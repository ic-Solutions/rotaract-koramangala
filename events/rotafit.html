ROTAFIT

Date

17th November 2019

Time

10:00 AM to 06:00 PM

Venue

Chaos Faktory

Attendees

126

Flow of Activities

🔹The event started with an enlightening Inauguration Ceremony followed by energetic warm up session.

🔸 Participants were briefed about the competition and the flow of events.

Level 1 Consisted of Basic Pushups, Squats, Jumping Over Boxes, Balancing on Beams and Burpees - Time Limit: 3 mins

🔹Many beginners and professional athletes along with kids participated, showing great dedication.

🔸Lunch -1:40 p.m to 3:00p.m.😋

🔹During lunch a Fun Karaoke Session was organised for everyone 🗣🎤

🔸3:00 p.m: First Round Results Announced

🔹Participants who completed circuit within 2 minutes 30 seconds were selected for the next round (18 Men and 7 Women participants qualified)

Level 2 consisted of Pyramid Climb, Pullups, Slug Push, Squats, Pushups to be completed within 4 mins💪🏼

🔸The final was followed by a Valedictory Session
