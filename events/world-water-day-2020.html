Flow of Activities

~ We Rotaract Club of Koramangala organised a Social Media Campaign on World Water Day to increase awareness about water conservation.

~ We prepared a creative to be shared by all participants and nominees

~ The creative included a few water conservation tips to follow in everyday life

~ Each participant took a pledge to conserve water & tagged 3/more friends to do the same

Project Highlights :

~ We were featured on SEARIC Instagram stories.

~ Our project is on UN's World Water Day website: https://www.worldwaterday.org/event/every-drop-counts-3/
