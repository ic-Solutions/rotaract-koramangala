Flow of Activities

~ As another Rotaract Year (July 18 - June 19) is about to end, we invited everyone to join us for our DOV.

~ Below activities as part of DOV were carried out :

1. On Display : All the club Reports and achievements for RIY 2018-19 till date was on display for everyone.

2. We took feedback from all present to improvise on our functioning.

3. People's Choice President 2019 - 20 : All members put there preference and Rtr. Anurag Patwary was chosen as People’s Choice President 2019 - 20.

4. Recent Achievement : Received RI Presidential Citation with Platinum Distinction for RIY 2018 - 19.
