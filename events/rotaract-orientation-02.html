Flow of Activities

~ In association with Rotaract club of Jyothi Nivas College Rtr.Sunil had given Rotaract orientation program to the more than 100 Rotaractors of Jyothi Nivas College.

~ During the Orientation- What is Rotaract Club? Origin of ‘Rotary in action’, The Goals of Rotaract Movement, What does Rotaractor Do? Main objectives of Four Avenues (Community, Professional, International & Club Services), Public Relations, How a Club works, Making a Event and Executing Event are covered.

~ Rotaractors also shared their personal Rotaract experience to inspire and motivate the students/ new members of Rotaract club of JNC.

~ Over all it was very good experience and learning session for newbies of Rotaract family.

~ Through the Joint project bonding between our sister clubs are stronger.
