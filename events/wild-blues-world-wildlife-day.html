Wild Blues - World Wildlife Day

Date

3rd March 2019

Time

NA

Venue

Bannergatta Park

Attendees

84

Attendees :

Shubham, Sanjay, Trisha, Syed, Ganesh, Srinidhi, Mitali, Shiv, Harish and Paramesh

Flow of Activities

~ Wildlife Briefing 🤠

~ Awareness about Trees, birds, insects ,animals & co-existence among different species 🎋🦅🐞🐘

~ Trekking 🐾

~ Documentary on Bannerghatta Breathing for Bangalore 🎥

~ Felicitating the Forest Officers 🕵🏻‍♂🎗

~ Gifting Trek Bags to 20 forest officials and First Aid Kits given to Bannerghatta National Park and Biological Park🎒⛑

~ Briefed local visitors about World Wildlife Day and had interactive sessions with the public to create awareness about conservation of wildlife and its habitat 🙇🏻‍♀

~ Wildlife hackathon 🗣

~ Forest Fire & Effects of plastic usage on Marine/Animal Life were the topics given to the teams and they came up with feasible solutions that could be easily implemented and the forest officials were happy with the suggestions received. We concluded that we should work together to bring about a change 🌊

~ Documentaries on Marine Life, Gaja and crocodile were also showcased🐳

~ Education Officers and outstanding participants were given T-shirts.

~ All the participants were given certificates and delicious breakfast. 📜🍛
