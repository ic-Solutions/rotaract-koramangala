Flow of Activities

~ Making the mark of our 70th republic day we decided to reach out to the common man, tell them about the life of our soldiers and to write a letter thanking them for all their service and sacrifices to us.

~ We got an opportunity to have a talk with many eminent personalities and got letters written from them. Few important ones were BBMP Mayor Gangambike, MLA Ramalingam Reddy, MLA Sowmya Reddy, BBMP Commissioner and many more We also got an opportunity to interact with many Rotary/Rotaract/Interact clubs across India who helped us with the project.

~ Total Clubs/Organization - 35; Total Letters collected - 4500
