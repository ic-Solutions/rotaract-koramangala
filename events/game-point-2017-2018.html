Flow of Activities

~ GamePoint v2.0 - is a fund raising flagship Club Service event of RC Koramangala. With 260+ participants and support of 20+ volunteer, we were able to complete the event successfully. Event was inaugurated by Rtn. Sunil Pai (IFBR Chair) and Rtn. PP Manivanan (Koramangala).

~ Event was supported by Rotaract Clubs of - West, Vijaynagar, Yelahanka, SBMJC, Jain University, AIMT, SIMS, Jayanagar BIT, Cubbon Park, Marathalli, PES University, Bishop Cottons, Mont Carmel & South Parade. We were graced with the presence of Mr. Vishal (Owner Spark Sports) and Rtr. Naveen Senna (DRR) who took time out from their busy schedule and contributed to our success. Event was concluded by Rtn. Janet (President Rotary Koramangala) and Rtr. Ravi Kumar (President RC Koramangala) who gave away winner prizes and shared vote of thanks to participants & volunteers.

~ Lastly, as decided, we would be utilizing the funds collected on the educational needs of Byragondlu village children.
