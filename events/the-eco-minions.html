The Eco Minions

Date

7th December 2019

Time

11:00 AM to 05:00 PM

Venue

Reforest India Nursery

Attendees

12

Event Details

🌳 We started the day with a quick introduction of members of RaCK and RFI volunteers.

🌳 The Team was divided into three groups .

🍃 One group Scrubbed and Fixed the cracks on the Wall .🛠

🍃 One group Painted Stools.🖌🎨

🍃 One group helped in Segregating and Organizing saplings and Watering them.🌱

🌳 We bonded over Scrumptious Lunch🥘🍛

🌳 We were explained the different Native Trees and how to look after them

🌳We got cuddled now and then by a bunch of cute Kittens🐱

It was a fun day of Volunteering, Learning , New Friendships and Membership Extension ☺
