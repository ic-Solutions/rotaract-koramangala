Club Outing at SRS Hills

Date

18th August 2019

Time

7:30 AM to 06:00 PM

Venue

SRS Hills (Ramnagar)

Attendees

20

Team Building activities with fun and treking at SRS Hill

Details of the event

~ All the team members gathered in the morning and started to SRS Hills for a long drive and had breakfast near Ramnagar. 🍛🥤 🔸

~ After reaching the destination, we started off with an ice breaking session making the Team comfortable with multiple activities. 🎭🤠 🔹

~ Once team is comfortable with each other we started trekking the hill. 🏃🏼‍♀🏃🏻 🔸

~ In the beautiful view of the mountains and lake, members have participated in the Team Communication Activity and Fun Events. 🤹‍♀ 🔹

~ Team has taken ample time to enjoy the nature and started trekking down. 🏂 🔸

~ We ended our day with some more fun activities and GBM -3 followed by Lunch. 🍱
