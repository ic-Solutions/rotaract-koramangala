Flow of Activities

~ GFCT – Grace Fellowship Charitable Trust is an orphanage which supports 120 kids. The orphanage takes care of the kid’s education and very minimum needs. It runs mainly on donations. The infrastructure of the orphanage is also bare minimum.

~ We got the request from the orphanage for painting and there we were painting all the inside wall on the main hall of the orphanage. All the kids were very excited to see us.

~ We also played some games with the kids and all of them were really happy till the end. We decided to come to this place during Christmas too.
