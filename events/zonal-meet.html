Zonal Meet

Date

1st September 2019

Time

09:00 AM to 11:00 AM

Venue

Cubbon Park

Attendees

8

Details of the event

~ We started with an introduction session of all the 11 clubs, their respective members and the ZRR & Co-ZRR. 🤘

~ The first activity was an icebreaker Bingo game that enabled all the members from different clubs to get to know each other in a very fun and interactive manner. 👯🏻‍♂👯🏼

~ Next few activities were competitive games against the other clubs where we learned to work together as a team and had loads of fun. 🤼‍♂🤼‍♀

~ The activities were followed by announcing the winners of all the games and club announcements. 🏆

~ We also had some fellowship and discussions about future activities & joint projects with all the clubs that had attended and made a few new friends along the way. 🤝

~ The event was followed by breakfast organised by RaC Koramangala and a general club discussion about future projects. 🍽
