Flow of Activities

~ As part of the Food Drive, we prepared 20 meal boxes at one of the Rotaractors home. The intention behind home made food was to provide one time healthy meal to the needy. We also bought six kgs of fruits from the local vendor.

~ We distributed meal boxes and fruits amongst 10 road side needy people. We then moved to the government hospital in Koramangala where we provided meals and fruits to 30 plus needy people. It was a great experience for all of us as we can see the happiness in the eyes of people while distributing. We look forward to take more initiatives like this.
