Flow of Activities

~ Did you know Rotary International immunizes over 2.5 Billion children every year?

~ Today, members of Rotaract club of Koramangala with our parent Rotary volunteered at the Government Health Centre in Adugodi.

~ We have immunized polio vaccine for 225 childrens on the day.

~ For 4 of our members it was 1st time being a part of an initiative like this, and they absolutely loved it. It was an amazing experience to administer the drops to cute-crying babies.
