Flow of Activities

~ GFCT – Grace Fellowship Charitable Trust is an orphanage which supports 120 kids. The orphanage takes care of the kid’s education and very minimum needs. It runs mainly on donations. The infrastructure of the orphanage is also bare minimum.

~ This was our third visit to the orphanage and we decided to engage more with kids this time. We divided them into teams and make them perform skit in the very basic topics about our country. Everyone was very motivated to do their part of the country.

~ Lastly we briefed everyone importance of our Soldiers and make them write letters to for their Guardians.
