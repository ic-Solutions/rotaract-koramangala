Flow of Activities

~ We joined our Parent Rotary (Rotary Club of Bangalore Koramangala) for Rotary Day along with RaC JNC. The aim was to engage in community service around our club area. We had Rotary Day for workers of Ejipura flyover who are building our locality roads to ease traffic.

~ Below Items were Distributed - 1. Old and new clothes 2. Food items including rice, dhal, wheat floor, oil etc. 3. Blankets and Bedsheets & 4. Grocery Items.

~ We also included the theme from our Distt. for this month i.e., No tobacco Day by a session on the day along with our support to these workers.

~ There were around 150+ workers majorly from Bihar region who were helped out here.

~ Overall we had very good time with our parent Rotary and Rotaract sister club and its helps us to stronger bonds with positive Rotaraction !!
