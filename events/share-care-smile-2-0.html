Flow of Activities

~ The members of Rotaract Club of Koramangala, joined together and contributed to raise funds for the project. We bought notebooks and pencils and distributed to around 60 students in the school. We also stressed the importance of using the notebooks wisely and not waste paper and had a few fun activities with the school kids.

~ It was altogether a wonderful experience for all of us. Spending time with kids is always fun and learning in a unique way. We thank Rotaract club of Hyderabad Himayathnagar for giving us this opportunity to conduct this beautiful project.
