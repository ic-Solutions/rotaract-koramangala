*Yuvraj Roy*, a young boy is currently studying in Class 3 at Dispur English Academy.

As any child of his age, he has big dreams and is very enthusiastic, dedicated and eager to learn new things everyday. 📚✏

Yuvraj has an older sister Jhintee, who is currently in Class 9.

His mother is a Homemaker and father is a Driver.

His parents are unable to afford to give him the quality education that he deserves.

But now, with our help, young Yuvraj is a step closer to His dreams of continuing his education._ 🤝🤗

This is the first phase in our new initiative, *Shiksha Seva*; where _we aim to support those in need to continue to follow their dreams._ 💫🙌
