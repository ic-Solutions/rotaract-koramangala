Dussehra Celebrations at BGMS || Project Report

Date

8th October 2019

Time

09:00 AM to 04:00 PM

Venue

BGMS Shishukunj Vidyalaya, Indiranagar

Attendees

257

Details of the event

👥 No. of Attendees from RaC Koramangala: 9

👥 No. of Attendees from RaC Indiranagar : 8

No. of Participating Children : 240

🔹We conducted four categories of cultural competitions :

1. Rangoli Contest 🌈🏼

2. Art Contest 🎨 - The topic given to the children was Festivals of India.

3. Singing Competition🎤 - The children performed under two categories - Solo and Group.

4. Dance Competition🕺🏼💃 - The children performed under three categories - Solo, Duet and Group.

🔹Both the children of BGMS and organising team of Rotaractors had lots of fun participating and watching all the performances. ☺🥳

🔹The judging for all the competitions was done by our Rotaractors . 👩🏻‍🏫 👨🏻‍🏫

🔹We concluded with a prize distribution :

Rangoli - There were 8 Teams out of which 3 teams won prizes .🏆

Art - One child from each age category received a Prize. 🏆

Singing - 5 Solo Singers and 1 Group won Prizes. 🏆

Dancing - 5 Solo dancers and One group were given Prizes.🏆
