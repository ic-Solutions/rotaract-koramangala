WinS and MHM Program

Date

09th February 2020

Time

10:30 AM to 01:00 PM

Venue

Cubbon Park

Attendees

16

Speaker for the session - 🗣

Rtn. Thirumuragan

Rtn. Malini

Event details : 📜

-> All of us assembled and had an introduction to Rotary International, WinS and the Speaker of the Session. 🙌🏽🏽

-> The speaker talked to us about Wash in Schools, the importance of following proper hygiene habits which was followed by a demonstration of correct way of hand washing by the Hand Washing volunteers among us. 🧴✋🏼🧼

-> The WinS session was followed by Menstrual Hygiene Management program where the girls learned about maintenance of good health during Period time. Rtn. Malini also gave a small talk about Sustainable Menstruation and demonstrated how to use a Cloth Pad. 🌸

-> Followed by a small discussion about cloth pads, Menstrual cups and Bleed Green 🌿 🔴 🌿 Session by Rtr. Aastha.

-> We summed up with a thanks giving to our speaker of the session. 🤝🏻

-> We would like to thank Zone Bendre for organising the event and all the members for their participation. 🤝🏻🙇‍♂
