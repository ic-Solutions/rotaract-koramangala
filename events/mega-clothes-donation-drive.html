Mega Clothes Donation Drive

Date

30th November 2019

Time

02:00 PM to 03:00 PM

Venue

RK Foundation, JC Road and Koramangala

Attendees

14

Details of the Event

🔹 Approximately 200 pieces of clothing were collected from the club members and their friends in between 18th to 25th November. 🥳🧺

🔸 Clothes were donated to the RK Foundation, an NGO which provides services in the impoverished communities of Bangalore Rural Areas. 🌟🤝🏼

🔸 Few clothes were also donated to construction workers and also to the Rotary Wall of Kindness. 🏵👷🏽
