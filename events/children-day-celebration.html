Flow of Activities

~ Marking the occasion of Children's Day Rotaract Club of Koramangala donated 62 pair of shoes to the school. We were joined by Rotaract Bangalore West who donated library books to the school and Rotaract Bangalore VijayNagar who donated stationary items to the kids. We were joined by Rotaract Bangalore Sheshadripuram & Rotaract Club of Shishu Mandir for various planned activities for entertaining and involving kids.

~ Rotaract Club of Koramangala created awareness among-st kids by making them doing skit along with Rotaractors on below topics. 1. Smoking/drinking, 2. Food wastage, 3. Saving water, 4. Child labour, 5. Importance of education, 6. How to handle an emergency, 7. Say no to begging, 8. Swach Bharat & 9. Impact of cell phones

~ Village Head and seniors members of the village recognized our efforts and they were appreciating the impact of Rotaract on society. As a token of thanks, we had an awesome village food and small visit to nearby village area by the villagers. Together we spend memorable time with kids & served a few hours for our Community..!!
