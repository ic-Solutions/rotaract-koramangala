Singing Bird Fest

Date

22nd December 2019

Time

10:30 AM

Venue

Rudraksha Farm, Kanakpura Rd.

Attendees

5

Flow of Activities

🔹 Previous Being Santa projects pictures (2017 and 2018) were displayed .📷

🔹 We interacted with the cute children and gave them and opportunity to write Christmas Greeting Cards for the orphan kids .

🎴🎴 The money raised from these Cards will be used to get goodies for the children. 🍡🍬🍫

🔹 We Showed them the list of requirements provided to us by the orphanage and Requested them to "Be a Santa for these children. 🎁🎁

🔹 We are glad to have received an overwhelming response 😍😍

🔹 A band agreed to play on Christmas Day Celebration to entertain the children ☺🎤🎸

🔹 Most of all we enjoyed the music and had a great time.
