Flow of Activities

~ Classes conducted – 4; Number of attendees - 40

~ Venue Partners - RaC SBMJEC, RaC JNC, RaC SSMRV, RaC Surana College

~ Trainers - Ms UMA (Professional Trainer for all from "SPEAKENG INDIA")

~ Rotaract Trainers - ZRR Krishnarjun, IPDRR Naveen Senna, Rtr. PP Sunil

~ The program has been really perceived by the people and many Non-Kannadigas has shown their interest. There is some inconsistency in participation as people have other commitments, however we are happy, and few learners are active throughout and have learned the language. Few can speak, and few are now getting the context which make them feel more connected to the language.

~ Online Initiative - 8 WhatsApp groups ; No. of Participants - 80

~ Every group has been assigned with Mentors & Observers.

~ We have received a great response from the people around for the catchy posts. We are glad about collaboration from many Rotaract clubs here which is working as an encouragement for all involved.

~ Sponsors - Speakeng India for Ms. UMA and Mr Manoj for 100 hand books on "Learn Kannada in 25 Days" which are given to all the participants attending.
