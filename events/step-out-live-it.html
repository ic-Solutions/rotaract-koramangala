Flow of Activities

~ Rtr. Ganesh took the initiative of making everyone fit before our fundraising project GAME POINT v3.0. A two weeks initiative was conducted with considering in mind - NO DIET..!!! TOTAL FUN..!!!! FIT THE EASY WAY!!!!

~ The 14 Days - 40KM challenge was planned in a way to make it achievable for all. Minimum distance to follow was : 16 Sep - 1.0km, 17 Sep - 1.5km, 18 Sep - 1.7km, 19 Sep - 2.1km, 20 Sep - 2.5km, 21 Sep - 3.0km, 22 Sep - 3.0km, 23 Sep - 3.5km, 24 Sep - 3.6km, 25 Sep - 4.0km, 26 Sep - 3.8km, 27 Sep - 3.5km, 28 Sep - 3.5km, 29 Sep - 3.0km

~ Every week Members are sent the screenshot of run status and some time they also shared live running activity also posted in the group for live cheer using the “Runtastic app”. Members also used other fitness apps and sent the screenshot/pics of their every activities.
