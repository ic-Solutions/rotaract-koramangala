Flow of Activities

~ Rototech is an annual science model making exhibition for high school students. We encouraged students of Government schools to research and understand scientific concepts. From that we encourage and support them to make models out of their creativity.

~ Members from various Rotaract clubs have participated in the event. Our club partnered with Govt. High School, Hanumantha Nagar, Bengaluru and bring 9 high school children who came to the event and presented their respective models. They also saw models prepared by other school children which definitely have been a learning for them in understanding science in a broader way.
