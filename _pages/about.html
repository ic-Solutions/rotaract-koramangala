The Face of Change

Rotaract Club of Koramangala is a community based club of youngsters (18 to 30 years of age).We organize and participate in projects with an aim to create a change in the society. We belong to Rotaray International District 3190 (operates in and around Bangalore,India) of Rotary International Foundation.
