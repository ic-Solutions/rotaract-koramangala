The Face of Change

Rotaract Club of Koramangala is a community based club of youngsters (18 to 30 years of age).We organize and participate in projects with an aim to create a change in the society. We belong to Rotaray International District 3191 (operates in and around Bangalore,India) of Rotary International Foundation.

Our Avenues of Service

We channel our commitment to service at home and abroad through four Avenues of Service, which are the foundation of any of our club activity :

Community Service

This avenue encourages every member to find ways to improve the quality of life for people in their communities and to serve the public interest.This avenue involves identification of causes, in one of the areas of focus, in order to plan and implement at least one activity or service project monthly, designed to create sustainable change that directly benefits the local or university community, involving all or most of the club membership.

Professional Development

This avenue focuses on a comprehensive professional development program on a monthly basis designed to expand professional networks, allow members to exchange ideas with other leaders, and build skills through leadership training. This avenue not only focuses on the professional development of our club members, but also to all outside our club.

International Service

This avenue focuses on exemplifying our global reach in promoting peace and understanding. We support this service avenue by sponsoring or volunteering on international projects, seeking partners abroad, visiting each other, cultural exchanges and more.

It is also designed to create sustainable change that directly benefits the international community, involving all or most of the club membership.

Club Service

It focuses on making clubs strong. A thriving club is anchored by strong relationships and an active membership development plan. We organize events to enhance bonding between members so as to grow and learn to work together as a team. These include club outings, fundraisers and other events that directly help and contribute to the club and its members.

This avenue also involves tracking attendance, developing membership, promoting activities, coordinating public relations, and other matters as appropriate.
